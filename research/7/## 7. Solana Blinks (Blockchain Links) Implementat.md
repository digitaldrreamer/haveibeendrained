## **‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Pitfalls to Avoid**

1. **Missing CORS headers** ‚Üí Blinks won't render in ANY client
2. **No actions.json at root** ‚Üí URLs won't map to actions
3. **requireAllSignatures: true** ‚Üí User can't sign
4. **No recentBlockhash** ‚Üí Transaction fails
5. **HTTP instead of HTTPS** ‚Üí Wallets reject URL
6. **Exposing secrets** ‚Üí Wallet compromise
