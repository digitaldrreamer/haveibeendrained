## Quick Start

Checking your wallet security is simple and free. Follow these steps:

<Steps>
<Step title="Visit the website">

Go to [haveibeendrained.org](https://haveibeendrained.org) in your browser.

</Step>

<Step title="Enter your wallet address">

Paste your Solana wallet address into the search field. You can find your wallet address in:
- Your wallet app (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
- Transaction history on Solscan or other explorers
- Your wallet's public key

</Step>

<Step title="Click Check">

Click the "Check Wallet" button to start the analysis. The process typically takes 10-15 seconds.

</Step>

<Step title="Review results">

View your risk score and any detected threats. Follow the recommendations to secure your wallet if needed.

</Step>
</Steps>

## What Gets Checked?

Our analysis examines your wallet's transaction history for:

1. **Known Drainers** - Addresses reported to our on-chain registry
2. **SetAuthority Attacks** - Unauthorized ownership transfers
3. **Unlimited Approvals** - Token approvals that allow unlimited spending
4. **Suspicious Patterns** - Unusual transaction clusters and sweeper bots

## Privacy & Security

<Info>
  **Your privacy matters:**
  - We never store your wallet address
  - We don't require any authentication
  - All analysis is performed in real-time
  - No private keys or sensitive data are accessed
</Info>
