This is called a **stealth launch rug pull**: the creator mines tokens for themselves before launch, waits for hype to pump the price, then exits with huge gains while retail losses pile up.

## Where the Scammer Won

<Warning>
- **Pre-mine:** 40% of tokens created at deploy, given to creator.
- **Stealth timing:** No pre-announcement means the creator gets in first, before anyone else.
- **No lock:** "Locked liquidity" was a false claim; the creator had full control and timing.
- **Hype channel:** Using Telegram "gem hunter" groups to flood new buyers with urgency.
</Warning>

## What Adanna Could Have Done (Prevention)

### Before Buying

<Check>**Check contract creation:** On BscScan, look at the token's creation block. Check:
- When was it created (less than 1 day old is risky)?
- What was the first transaction (did the creator mint a large % of tokens)?
- Is the creator's wallet visible on the holders list?</Check>

<Check>**Check holder distribution:** On BscScan, view "Token Holders." If one wallet holds > 20% (other than the liquidity pool), assume it's a dump risk.</Check>

<Check>**Verify "locked liquidity":** Even if they claim liquidity is locked, verify on Pinksale, Unicrypt, or the contract. A claim without proof is worthless.</Check>

<Check>**Be skeptical of "stealth launches."** The mechanics are designed to benefit insiders over retail. Real projects announce launches publicly and give everyone equal access.</Check>

### If You Bought Anyway

<Check>**Exit on the first major dump.** If price drops > 20% in one candle, don't hold hoping for recovery. Get out.</Check>

<Check>**Track large wallet movements.** If the creator's wallet shows massive sells, that's your signal to exit immediately.</Check>

## Red Flags Checklist

<Warning>
- [ ] **Token created < 1 day ago.**
- [ ] **One wallet holds > 20% of supply.**
- [ ] **"Stealth launch" = insiders got in before public announcement.**
- [ ] **Locked liquidity claimed but not verifiable on-chain.**
- [ ] **Large sudden sell orders from early wallets.**
- [ ] **Hype comes from Telegram "gem hunter" channels, not official announcements.**
</Warning>

## How Stealth Launch Rug Pulls Work

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1: Pre-Mine" icon="coins">
**What happens:** Creator deploys contract and mints large percentage of tokens to their own wallet before public launch.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Step 2: Stealth Launch" icon="rocket">
**What happens:** Token is launched without pre-announcement. Creator and insiders buy first at lowest price.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Step 3: Hype Phase" icon="megaphone">
**What happens:** Telegram channels and social media hype the token. Retail buyers flood in, driving price up.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Step 4: Dump" icon="arrow-down">
**What happens:** Once price is high enough, creator dumps their pre-mined tokens in one large transaction.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Step 5: Collapse" icon="trending-down">
**What happens:** Price crashes 80-99%. Retail holders cannot exit due to low liquidity and high slippage.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Action Steps

### Prevention

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check Contract Age" icon="clock">
**Rule:** If token is less than 1 day old, be extremely cautious. Check creation block and first transactions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Verify Holder Distribution" icon="chart-pie">
**Check:** Use BscScan/EtherScan to view top holders. If creator holds > 20%, it's a dump risk.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Avoid Stealth Launches" icon="ban">
**Rule:** Stealth launches are designed to benefit insiders. Prefer projects with public, fair launches.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Set Stop-Loss" icon="target">
**Rule:** If you do buy, set a stop-loss. Exit immediately if price drops > 20% in one candle.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### If You're Already Involved

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check On-Chain Immediately">
    Verify contract age, holder distribution, and creator wallet activity.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Exit on First Major Dump">
    If price drops > 20% suddenly, exit immediately. Don't hold hoping for recovery.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor Creator Wallet">
    Track the creator's wallet for sell activity. If they're selling, exit.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Report the Token">
    Report to ChainAbuse and warn others in Telegram channels.
  </Step>
</Steps>
