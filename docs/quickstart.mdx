## For Users

<Steps>
<Step title="Visit the website">

Go to [haveibeendrained.com](https://haveibeendrained.com) in your browser.

</Step>

<Step title="Enter your wallet address">

Paste your Solana wallet address into the search field.

</Step>

<Step title="View results">

Get your risk score and security recommendations in seconds.

</Step>
</Steps>

<Card title="User Guide" icon="book" href="/user-guide/getting-started">
  Learn more about checking your wallet and understanding results.
</Card>

## For Developers

<Steps>
<Step title="Make your first API call">

```bash
curl "https://api.haveibeendrained.com/api/v1/check?address=WALLET_ADDRESS" \
  -H "User-Agent: MyApp/1.0.0 (contact@example.com)"
```

</Step>

<Step title="Understand the response">

The API returns either a drainer report or wallet analysis with risk score.

</Step>

<Step title="Integrate into your app">

Use our REST API or embeddable widget in your application.

</Step>
</Steps>
